About Us

Working together to create a changeâ€¦

During the lockdown, when we were stuck in our homes, the street animals were not getting any food to eat. We started feeding the dogs in our locality every day. Eventually, we realised the various problems the animals in the street were facing. So, we dedicatedly started helping all those in need and eventually grew together as a team to form The Stray Army Charitable Trust. We believe that every animal deserves a second chance so that they can live their lives with love, care and affection. Our programmes include:

  • Daily Feeding Drives
  • Winter Drives
  • Reflected Collar Drives
  • Anti-Rabies Drives
  • Adoption Carnival
  • Water Bowl Drives
