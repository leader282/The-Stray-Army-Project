What we do

Working now and forever for the well-being of animals including rescue, care, medical treatment, adoption, awareness events and many more...

Our mission is to rescue and treat the unowned street animals of Jamshedpur, who have become ill or injured, and through their rescue, inspire a community to protect and defend the lives of all animals. We believe all animals deserve compassion and protection. We are working for the day when all animals' lives will be respected for their inherent value, whether dogs, chickens or fish
